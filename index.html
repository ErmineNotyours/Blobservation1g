Blobservation

Concept by docgunthrop, Programed by Arthur Allen.

A challenge from Codewars

A cellular automation not unlike Conway's Life. Blobs of various sizes are placed around a room. Each blob will take a step towards the nearest smaller blob and will engulf it when it occupies the same cell. The smallest size blob(s) can't move. When multiple valid blobs of equal size and distance are away from the predatory blob, blob is supposed to give priority to the first angle from 12:00 and then moving in a clockwise direction, but in practice the opposite is true.

This is a tricky and opaque challenge, and I needed to write this animated version to debug and see where I went wrong.
